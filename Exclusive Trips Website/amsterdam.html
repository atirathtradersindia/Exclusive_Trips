About Amsterdam

Amsterdam — the city of canals and creativity — blends centuries-old charm with a modern, artistic vibe. From cruising along the waterways to exploring world-class museums, Amsterdam offers an unforgettable blend of culture, freedom, and beauty. Enjoy local markets, cycling tours, and café-lined streets that make every moment postcard-perfect.

Sample itinerary

Day 1 — Arrival & Canal Cruise
Arrive in Amsterdam and relax with an evening canal cruise under illuminated bridges.
Day 2 — Museums & History
Visit Rijksmuseum, Van Gogh Museum, and Anne Frank House.
Day 3 — Tulip Fields & Windmills
Excursion to Keukenhof Gardens and Zaanse Schans windmills.
Day 4 — Bicycles & Markets
Explore the city like a local — cycle through Jordaan and visit Albert Cuyp Market.
