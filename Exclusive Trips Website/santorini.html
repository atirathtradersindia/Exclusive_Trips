About Santorini

Santorini is a volcanic island in the Aegean Sea known for dramatic views, stunning sunsets, and the whitewashed houses of Oia. Our curated package includes transfers, a boutique hotel, a sunset cruise and guided tours of ancient sites. You’ll have free time to explore local cuisine and unwind on the island’s unique volcanic beaches.
